Three-stage cleaning for maximum efficiency

The only thing Winbot does is clean windows, leaving them shining and spotless. First, the front Cleaning Pad, sprayed with cleaning solution, moistens, loosens and absorbs dirt. Second, the Squeegee draws the remaining water-borne dirt and dampness off the window. Third the rear Cleaning Pad wipes the window to dry, spotless condition
