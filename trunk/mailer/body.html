Milagrow Wheeme: Like a Roomba for your sore back

Why is Milagrow Wheeme so amazing? The state-of-the-art Wheeme is the first commercial robot of its kind, capable of navigating gently and safely on human body without falling off in most cases.
Is it suitable for me? Milagrow Wheeme is designed for adults, teen and children. It is perfectly safe and its light weight doesn't pressure sensitive body parts. Since minimal operation is required, it is not intended for children under 5 years

VIBRATION WheeMe vibrates as it moves. The vibrations from the wheels transfer to the skin, helping to ease aches and strains and revitalizing the body by releasing stress along the spine.
TINGLING Tiny finger-like projections tingle the back and wake up the senses. Just mount the "Tingle Fingers" to WheeMe for a delightfully fun tingling sensation that's as relaxing as remedial.
CARESS WheeMe moves up and down the back in soft, fluid motions. Without any vibrations, the ridged rubberized wheels soothe the back and dissolve stress in a matter of minutes.
